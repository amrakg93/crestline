## CLASS 10 — BOARD EXAM

### Mathematics (Semester-wise — Fully MCQ from 2025-26)
**Semester 1 (40 marks):** Commercial Mathematics (GST, Banking) · Algebra: Linear Inequations, Quadratic Equations, Ratio & Proportion, Factorisation, Matrices, Arithmetic Progression · Geometry: Similarity

**Semester 2 (40 marks):** Algebra: Coordinate Geometry · Geometry: Circles (Angle, Tangent, Secant, Cyclic Properties, Constructions) · Mensuration (Cone, Sphere, Cylinder) · Trigonometry (Expressions, Heights & Distances) · Statistics (Mean, Median, Mode, Histograms, Ogive) · Probability (Sample space, Random experiments, Events)

### Physics (6 Units — 80 Theory + 20 Practical)
| Unit | Weightage | Key Topics |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Force, Work, Power & Energy | 16-19 marks | Uniform Circular Motion, Turning Forces, Gravity, Work/Power/Energy, Machines, Conservation of Energy |
| Light | 20-25 marks | Refraction, Electromagnetic Spectrum, Total Internal Reflection, Lenses |
| Sound | 11-18 marks | Vibrations & Resonance, Reflection of Sound, Loudness/Pitch/Quality |
| Electricity & Magnetism | 20-21 marks | Ohm's Law, Electrical Power/Energy, Household Circuits, Magnetic Effect of Current |
| Heat | 14-18 marks | Latent Heat, Calorimetry, Loss and Gain of Heat |
| Modern Physics | 7-11 marks | Radiation, Nuclear Energy, Radioactivity |

### Chemistry (9 Chapters — 80 Theory + 20 Practical)
1. **Periodic Properties** — Periodic trends, Periodicity by atomic number
2. **Chemical Bonding** — Electrovalent, covalent, coordinate bonding
3. **Acids, Bases, Salts** — Definitions, Ions, Salt types, Preparation
4. **Analytical Chemistry** — NaOH/NH₄OH action on salts, Alkali on metals/oxides
5. **Mole Concept & Stoichiometry** — Gas laws, Vapour density, Mole calculations
6. **Electrolysis** — Electrolytes, Ion migration, Applications
7. **Metallurgy** — Metal extraction, Aluminium, Alloys & Amalgams
8. **Study of Compounds** — HCl, Ammonia, Nitric Acid, Sulphuric Acid
9. **Organic Chemistry** — Intro, Isomerism, Homologous series, Nomenclature, Hydrocarbons, Alcohols, Carboxylic acids
